Oven Baked Chicken Wings

Description

A game day classic. These oven baked chicken wings have a simple but delicious seasoning of paprika, garlic, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 10 thawed chicken wings, tips removed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil. Dot the foil with the butter, there is no need to melt it ahead of time but you can if you would like.
  3. In a medium sized bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt and pepper.
  4. oat both sides of each wing fully in the flour mixture and place on the baking sheet. Make sure the butter pieces are evenly spaced out among the wings.
  5. Bake wings for 30 minutes.
  6. Turn wings over and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until crispy.
  7. Serve with your favourite dipping sauce and sprinkle with fresh parsley if desired
  8. Enjoy.
